TITLE: Tunnel Vision: A ludique structure for the underground
ADVISOR: Theodora Vardouli

Montreal’s underground is a paradoxical infrastructure. While its architecture is distinct from, yet deeply connected to, the city at grade. Altogether, these elements create an urban dramaturgy that overwhelms utilitarian, choreographed flows, and shifting proximities of pressure, artificial atmospheres; choreographed flows, and shifting proximities of pressure, artificial atmospheres.
